Major Health Concerns For Every Teen

Personal Hygiene

Personal hygiene is an essential aspect of overall health and well-being. Teaching teens about proper hygiene practices, such as regular handwashing, oral hygiene, bathing/showering, and skincare, can help prevent the spread of germs, infections, and skin problems. These are practical and easy-to-implement habits that promote good health and prevent illness.

Learn more

Nutrition and Healthy Eating

Educating teens about nutrition and healthy eating habits can empower them to make informed food choices and maintain a balanced diet. Teaching them about the importance of consuming a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and limiting processed foods, sugary drinks, and excessive salt can help prevent diet-related issues like obesity, malnutrition, and chronic diseases.

Learn more

Physical Activity and Exercise

Regular physical activity is crucial for maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Encouraging teens to engage in at least 60 minutes of moderate-to-vigorous physical activity daily can help improve cardiovascular health, build strength, enhance mental well-being, and prevent obesity. Teaching them about different types of exercises, the benefits of staying active, and how to incorporate physical activity into their daily routines can have a positive impact on their overall health.

What We Do

Mentorship Registrations and Pairing

Project TEAM has recruited medical student mentors from the University of California, Irvine School of Medicine to guide and support younger students in their pursuit of health professional careers. The program was established by UCI Family Medicine residents with the aim of teaching and mentoring middle school and high school students.The program has a partnership with Orange County School of Arts (OCSA), a public charter school located in Downtown Santa Ana. By joining as a mentee, students will have the opportunity to interact with healthcare professionals, ask questions, receive guidance, and seek educational opportunities.
